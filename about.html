Who is BayleeBug Designs?

As a grandmother to 5 boys and 4 girls, when little Baylee came along after the first two grandsons were born, my creative juices started flowing with all the new possibilities having a girl in the family meant. From there, and many dresses, aprons, and play clothes later, BayleeBug Designs was born. BayleeBug was the nickname given to my beautiful granddaughter, and from the time she was born it has stuck. She was my first inspiration for these dresses and items, and she still inspires me today, along with my other granddaughters Zoey ,Ruby, and Mackenzie as well as my grandsons, Aidan, Jacob, Austin, Jordon, and Nathan. Now about me, I love to sew, and my two main passions are quilting and children's items. I believe in quality, so I work very hard on making the most beautiful and practical items from my home.
